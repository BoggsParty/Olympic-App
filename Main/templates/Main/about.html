What is happening here?

This is a website for my friends and family to have a little competition to see who can make the best guesses for a few sport events at the Olympics. This is also the first website I have ever made.

Why?

Mostly I did this to practice making a website and to get people more interested in the Summer Olympics.

What is the prize?

1st Place: Ownership of the Champion trophy until the Winter Olympics, a first place medal to keep, 1 trip with Julia to the aquarium (redeem by April 20th, 2017) and dinner with Julia at a restaurant of your choosing with a $$ or less on Yelp.
2nd Place: A second place medal to keep and 1 trip with Julia to the Shedd Aquarium (redeem by April 20th, 2017)
3rd Place: A third place medal to keep

How does scoring work?

1 exactly correct guess: 10pts
2 exactly correct guesses: 25pts
3 exactly correct guesses: 50pts
Choosing a winning team but not the correct place: 5 pts

When is the deadline?

All guesses must be made by 11:59 CST on August 5th (the day of the Opening Ceremony) Scores will be updated within 24hrs of the medal rounds for each events.

Who chose the sports?

I did, trying to keep a good mix of team and individual sports, popular sports and interesting sports.
